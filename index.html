Research identity

Robotic Perception and Georeferenced Intelligence Lab

The lab vision is to build intelligent sensing systems that combine electronics, signal processing, computer vision, SLAM, GNSS/IMU/camera fusion, and embedded AI for outdoor robotic deployment.

01

Georeferenced SLAM

Metric-scale 3D mapping with camera trajectories anchored by GNSS priors, factor graphs, and robust optimization.

02

Field robotics

Perception systems for orchards, mines, roads, farms, infrastructure corridors, and industrial inspection sites.

03

Semantic landmarks

Using stable objects such as apples, trunks, poles, terrain structures, and assets as constraints for robust mapping.

04

Embedded AI

Real-time deployment on edge hardware using stereo cameras, Jetson platforms, GNSS receivers, IMUs, and efficient models.
