Computer Vision and Robotic Perception

A practical elective that links core image processing and vision theory to modern field robotics and autonomous inspection systems.

  • Image formation and camera models
  • Filtering, edges, and frequency-domain interpretation
  • Feature detection, matching, and geometric vision
  • Stereo vision and depth estimation
  • Visual odometry and SLAM
  • Deep learning for detection and segmentation
  • Vision foundation models for 3D perception
  • Semantic mapping and landmarks
  • Embedded deployment on Jetson / edge devices
  • Mini-project: field perception pipeline
